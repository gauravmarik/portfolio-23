Want to hop on a ride that is cheaper than Uber?

Here is the RideX App with similar features and functionalities as found in the Uber App. Upon selecting pick up & drop off locations/cities, it fetches coordinates from mapbox API and displays locations on the map with a list of the available rides & ride cost.
